Terms of Use

Welcome to the ETIB Community Connect Directory. By using this site, you agree to abide by our community guidelines and use the directory for informational and connection purposes only. ETIB does not guarantee the quality of services listed. You are responsible for verifying any service details and accessibility needs directly with providers.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This directory collects only the information necessary to connect users with businesses. We do not sell or share personal data. Your use of contact forms and direct communication with listed businesses is at your discretion.

Accessibility Statement

ETIB is committed to making this directory accessible for blind and visually impaired users. Our goal is to meet or exceed WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact us so we can improve your experience.

Community Listing Standards

All businesses listed must be blind or visually impaired-owned, or serve blind and visually impaired individuals. Listings must clearly explain how they support our community, provide complete contact details, and commit to accessible service. ETIB reserves the right to remove any listing that does not align with these standards.

Contact & Support

If you have questions, concerns, or need support using this directory, please reach out to us at eventhoughimblind@gmail.com or call 910-853-5247. We value your feedback and aim to provide a welcoming, accessible environment for everyone.
