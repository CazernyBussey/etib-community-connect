About this directory

ETIB Community Connect Directory is a mission-driven connection hub. No payments are processed. Users connect directly with providers by phone, text, email, or website.

ETIB Community Connect Directory provides a community directory for informational and connection purposes only. ETIB does not process payments or guarantee third-party services. Users should contact providers directly to confirm service details and accessibility needs.
